COLLAVORATIVE INNOVATION CENTER -CIC-

IBM and the Center for Advanced Studies Benelux create powerful collaborations with universities and the government, setting as their ultimate goal the co-creation of research, start-ups and education, accessible to all IBMers, clients and students.

IBM and CAS have the chance to showcase their capabilities to clients and the outside world as well as open paths for the internal organization and employees towards education and innovation.

What are Watson’s capabilities? How far can it go and can we train it on a certain topic?

This partnership with VU Amsterdam aims to create a CIC on Watson education and to explore what possibilities lie ahead by using the IBM Watson technology. This collaboration strives to understand how we can use cognitive technology and how are can make Watson accessible to all IBMers

CIC on Big Data Science

Big Data is everywhere and seen as one of the biggest IT challenges regarding its economic and societal impact. IBM Netherlands and the Center for Advanced Studies Benelux take action by establishing an important partnership with TU Delft, through which joint research is conducted to identify how to accelerate Big Data applications by means of hardware and software components and make use of them for social and economic purposes. Using IBM Bluemix this partnership promotes research on the domains of Data Science, Internet of Things and Water – one of the most important resources on our planet. One of these programs is Inclusive Enterprise.
